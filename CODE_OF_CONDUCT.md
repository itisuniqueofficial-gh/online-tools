# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to maintaining a respectful and productive project environment for contributors and users.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus feedback on the work, not the person.
- Assume good intent while asking for clarification when needed.
- Help keep discussions relevant to the project.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or behavior
- Publishing private information without permission
- Spam, trolling, or intentionally disruptive conduct

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior does not meet these standards.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns through GitHub repository discussions or issues when appropriate:

https://github.com/itisuniqueofficial-gh/online-tools/discussions
